Mother Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to support life. Its unique combination of land, water, and atmosphere creates a diverse range of ecosystems and climates. The planet is characterized by its vibrant blue oceans, lush green forests, and vast deserts.

The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and is the fifth-largest moon in the solar system.The Moon's surface is covered in craters, mountains, and vast plains known as "maria," formed by ancient volcanic activity.

The Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of our solar system and is the primary source of energy for life on Earth. Composed mostly of hydrogen (about 74%) and helium (about 24%), it generates energy through nuclear fusion, converting hydrogen into helium and releasing vast amounts of light and heat.
